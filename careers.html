    • Job Title: QA Automation

    Job Posting: 03/01/2019

    Job ID: GI15001

    Job Description:

    • Responsible for activities involving quality assurance and compliance with applicable regulatory requirements
    • Develops and implements total quality management (TQM) programs and initiatives
    • Develops, implements, and leads key process improvement efforts and influences cross-functional efforts
    • Assists in the development of systematic approaches for assuring high quality services
    • Makes continual improvement of products, processes and systems
    • Requirements analysis, test case development and test execution for new software applications as well as application enhancements
    • Creates and maintains test automation frameworks across multiple projects
    • Researches Change Requests as well as regression testing of multiple applications
    • Works closely with project managers, business users, and software development team.

    REQUIREMENTS:

    • Minimum of a Bachelor’s degree or its equivalent or higher in Computer Science, Information Systems – Information Assurance or Information Technology.
    • At least 5+ years of experience in Software Testing & Quality Assurance, required
    • 3+ years of experience writing / maintaining test automation frameworks using Selenium highly desired
    • Must have ability to translate needs of business users to technical staff
    • Experience working with Microsoft TFS (Team Foundation Server)
    • Proficient experience with writing SQL queries
    • Strong analytics skills to determine weak points in application and hardware through measurement of service level agreements and documented requirements
    • Must have solid understanding of application testing, database, with an understanding of the agile development methodology with ability to own defect management / resolution
    • Solid experience writing, reviewing, and implementing Test Plans (TP) and Test Completion Summary (TCS) documentation with quality control, project, and technical teams
    • Tool experience for developing functional and performance automation validation
    • Proven ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment is required
    • Must be flexible, well organized and able to work well under pressure

    • Mobile Application Testing

    • Microsoft Test Manager

    • Load Testing, preferred

    • Experience working in Agile Scrum methodology is desirable
